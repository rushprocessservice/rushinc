About

Rush Process Service, Inc.
Est. 1992

Established by Michael Kennedy with the goal of providing exceptional and cost effective services for legal professionals, we are proud to say that we have succeeded in that goal and have grown to be one of the largest process serving companies in the state of Oregon.

In addition to our experienced in-house staff, we have over a dozen professional process servers and retrieval specialists in the field. By utilizing our expertise and extensive network, we are able to cover the entire state of Oregon and provide the same quality service wherever it is needed.

For your convenience, daily trips are made to the Federal County Courts, Oregon Corporations, Registered Agents, State Agencies, and Medical Facilities, and residential stops and priority documentation can be picked up and delivered to the courts the same day.

We look forward to providing new customers with the same quality of service that our established clients have come to know and trust.

RUSH Process Service, Inc. are proud members of the National Association of Professional Process Servers, and the Oregon Association of Process Servers.
